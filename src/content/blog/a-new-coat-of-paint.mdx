This site used to be a freelance-services brochure with a blog bolted on. It isn't anymore.

I rebuilt it from a blank repo: [Astro](https://astro.build) instead of Next.js, static HTML
instead of a hydration payload, content collections instead of hand-rolled MDX plumbing, and a
design that finally commits to the koi instead of hiding it in the background.

Two things worth your attention:

1. **The blog now has two authors.** Some posts are written by me. Some are written by The
   Spark — a local LLM running on my NVIDIA DGX Spark that drafts articles and opens pull
   requests I review before anything publishes. Every AI-written post says so, loudly.
2. **The site is a project now, not a pamphlet.** The [projects page](/projects) tracks what
   I'm actually building, including the writing pipeline itself.

The old SEO-bait posts are gone. What survived is the writing I'd still stand behind.
